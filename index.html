PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: November 16, 2024

This Privacy Notice for Safe Path Prosperity INC ("we", "us", or "our") describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Salamati mobile application.

What Information Do We Collect?

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Age

How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your information to create and manage accounts, provide app features, allow chat functionality, manage hygiene kit pickup orders, and communicate with users.

How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We keep your information while your account is active. When your account is deleted, your data is retained for 14 days in case you choose to restore your account. After 14 days, your account and associated personal data are permanently deleted from our systems.

Account Deletion

To delete your account, open the Salamati app and go to: Settings → Edit Profile → Delete Account.

If you log back in within 14 days, your account will be restored and the deletion request cancelled.
